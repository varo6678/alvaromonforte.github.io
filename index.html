Graph Neural Network for Predicting Molecular Properties:
Developed a Graph Neural Network (GNN) model to analyze the scalar coupling constant (SCC) in molecular interactions. Utilized graph theory and adjacency matrices to represent molecular structures. Implemented the model using TensorFlow, Google Cloud Platform, and the NVIDIA Tesla P100 GPU to handle large datasets and improve model accuracy.

Initiation to the theoretical study of materials: DFT for C, Si, Ge and Sn

Theoretical Study of Materials using Density Functional Theory (DFT):
Investigated the properties of Carbon (C), Silicon (Si), Germanium (Ge), and Tin (Sn) in cubic and hexagonal diamond structures. Applied Vienna ab initio Simulation Package (VASP) and Birch-Murnaghan equations of state to compute electronic properties and analyze the relationship between volume and energy under compression. Compared results with published calculations, contributing to the understanding of different diamond structures and potential applications in material discovery.
